ObserVIBElity

Click-to-deploy AI observability demo

ObserVIBElity packages a small e-commerce frontend (NeonCart) plus a support assistant on top of an LLM gateway, Postgres, and an OpenTelemetry collector wired to Grafana Cloud — so you get a realistic multi-service AI app with end-to-end telemetry from one install.

This wizard runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Pick a path to get started.
